Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do your shirts come in?

Our tees are available in sizes S through XXL based on availability.

Where do you ship?

We currently ship within the U.S., but we’re working on expanding to international shipping soon!

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5–10 business days depending on your location.

Can I return or exchange items?

We offer local exchanges for sizing issues within 14 days of delivery. Reach out to us via email to start the process.
