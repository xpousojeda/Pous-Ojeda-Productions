Our Founder

Xavier Pous-Ojeda — also known as Chemical X — is the creative force and visionary behind Pous-Ojeda Productions. Born in the Midwest with a deep passion for music, culture, and community, Xavier has dedicated his life to building a platform that goes beyond entertainment.

Inspired by artists like Mac Miller, Kanye West, and A$AP Rocky, Xavier blends storytelling and sonics to deliver more than just sound — he delivers a feeling. From beat making and engineering to brand building and grassroots collaboration, he has made authenticity the heart of the work.

Pous-Ojeda Productions is a reflection of his values: integrity, expression, and empowerment. Through each track, project, and tee — Xavier’s mission is to uplift and inspire the next generation of independent creatives.
